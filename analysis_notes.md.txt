About Dataset:

This dataset is a synthetically generated customer behavior and churn dataset designed using business-driven statistical distributions and behavioral logic to approximate real-world subscription-based customer dynamics.
 It simulates ustomer demographics, subscription characteristics, engagement behavior, support interactions, and payment patterns to enable realistic churn analysis while ensuring full privacy and ethical compliance.
 No personally identifiable nformation (PII) or real company data is included.

DATA OVERVIEW:
Total Records: 15,000
Total Features: 21
Target Variable: churn
0 = Retained
1 = Churned

Feature Categories:

Customer Demographics:
age
gender
region
income_level (low / medium / high)

Subscription & Billing Information:
subscription_type (basic / standard / premium)
signup_date
payment_method
monthly_charges
total_spent

Behavioral & Engagement Metrics:
usage_frequency
average_session_duration
last_login_days
support_tickets(Number of time a customer contacted the customer support)
satisfaction_score (1-5)

Engagement & Retention Signals:
discount_usage (yes / no)
promotional_response (yes / no)


Observation Window:
The dataset simulates customer behavior across a 12-24 month subscription lifecycle.
